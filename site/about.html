About Carolina Climate Control

From one truck to thirty employees — built on trust, hard work, and a love for Charleston.

How It All Started

Carolina Climate Control was founded in 2014 by Marshall Gay, an Atlanta transplant who had been working in the HVAC industry since his teenage years. After nine years of honing his craft, Marshall made the leap and started his own company — literally out of the back of his truck.

What began as a one-man operation has grown into one of Charleston's most trusted HVAC companies, with over 30 employees and 15 service trucks covering the entire tri-county area. Through it all, the mission has stayed the same: deliver honest, reliable service that puts the customer first.

The company mascot, Mr. Moose (Marshall's French bulldog), represents the friendly, caring approach that defines the Carolina Climate Control experience.
